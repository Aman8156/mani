Mani singh Biography

Born: January 28, 1995

Town: Khanpur

Hoshiarpur, State of Punjab, India Mani Singh immigrated to the United States in the year 2003 at the age of 7 with family. He grew up in the city of Santa Clara, California. He began his journey in the entertainment industry shortly after he graduated from John Casablanca Modeling and Talent Agency. To expand his horizon in the fashion industry he took part in multiple Fashion Shows throughout Northern California. Perfect Wedding Guide Fashion Show was his first show that took place in Sacramento, California. Through the use of his acquired network he was able to shift into the world of entertainment. In late 2014, he was acquainted with the Bay Area rapper and producer Haji Springer. Haji Springer saw potential in Mani from the very beginning and decided to Star Mani in one of his videos. “Koi Ni Parwa” is where it all took off for him. That music video opened many doors for Mani Singh in the Indian Music Industry. Haji Springer introduced Mani to Bohemia, aka King of Desi Hip Hop, who owns KDM (Kali Denali Music). As of today, Mani has done multiply music videos with the KDM team and is consistently growing.
